About the Fund

The National Infrastructure Fund (INFRA) is the Kingdom’s lead developmental financing partner for infrastructure. It facilitates higher levels of private sector investment to accelerate the delivery of infrastructure projects critical to the Kingdom’s ongoing social and economic transformation.

INFRA partners with local and global investors to build long term depth in the Kingdom’s infrastructure financing markets. Its specialist expertise supports innovation across all sectors and project types.

INFRA operates under a professional investment framework to ensure its financial sustainability over the long term.

As a developmental impact investor, it prioritizes projects with positive social and economic outcomes, and it is committed to increasing investment in the Kingdom’s sustainable infrastructure to ensure a green and prosperous future.

INFRA is a supervised entity of the National Development Fund.
